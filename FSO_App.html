Departments

Office Location

Travel km is estimated as a round trip from this office. Capture the office GPS once while at the office.

GPS distance is straight-line; road km is usually 1.2–1.4× more (higher through mountain passes). The estimate is always a guide — confirm against the odometer for claims.

Stores / Material Link

Paste a public/published link to your stores workbook (e.g. a published Google Sheet or a OneDrive link set to "anyone with the link"). The material Search button reads from it.

SharePoint

Job registration page

The page where a new job is registered on SharePoint (where the official FSO number is issued and the job folder is created). The "Register job ↗" buttons open this link. If the URL ever changes, just update it here.

Company system emails

Used by the "Create on Company System" button — one email with the signed PDF, sent from your own mailbox to Admin, with the Service Manager in CC.

Upload exports to SharePoint
Upload PDF/xlsx to the job folder

When ON, every export is also uploaded into the FSO's pasted SharePoint job folder (in addition to the OneDrive copy). Requires IT to grant the Sites.ReadWrite.All permission on the app registration — leave OFF until that's approved, then sign out and back in to OneDrive so the new permission is picked up.

Sheet creation

      

        
        
      

      
“As needed” builds each sheet when you first send that FSO (instant book creation). “All up front” builds every sheet when the book is created.

    

    

      

        Your FSO workbooks and data live in your own OneDrive. To connect, register a
        free personal app at entra.microsoft.com → App registrations (Single-page
        application, personal Microsoft accounts), set the redirect URI to this app's web address, and
        paste the Application (client) ID above. The app works offline either way and
        syncs when you're back online.
